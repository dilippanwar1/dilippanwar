About

Let me introduce myself.

I started my journey by ditching IBM and joining Tech Mahindra as an Associate Technical Support and worked for almost 2.5 years for AT&T. I also worked as a trainer for graduate interns in TechM for Java Projects.
After TechM, I got an opportunity to work with Gale Technologies in 2011 (now a part of Dell). In Gale, I got hitched with Cloud Computing domain and wrote Automation Framework, adapters/plugins to automate/orchestrate Compute, Network, Storage and Virtualization resources. I also worked as a Backend Java Developer for Eclipse Based Product. After the Gale acquisition by Dell, I worked on a Green Field Project(Dell Active System Manager) based on Java/J2EE and Puppet. I wrote Puppet modules and CRUD services for ASM.
I joined Citi Bank as a DevOps Engineer in 2014 and worked to migrate Datacenter from TPM (IBM Tivoli Provisioning Manager) to Chef and Java Based Product. I also worked on ESB(TIBCO) API development and handled production issues.
In 2015, near to Independence day, I left Citi Bank and joined Snapdeal, I worked on Automating Snapdeal Infrastructure using Chef Jenkins and AWS. Due to unstability of Snapdeal, I decided to checkout from Snapdeal in almost three months.
In Nov'2015 I joined hCentive Technologies as a Lead DevOps Engineer and designed a Configuration based framework to manage the Infrastructure. I developed a CI/CD pipeline using Jenkins, AWS and Chef. I also worked as a Sheriff for Infrastructure security and Audit purpose.
